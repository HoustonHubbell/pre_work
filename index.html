About Me

Hello , my name is Houston but my friends and family call me Hootie. A lifelong nickname. I am from Minnesota and received an associates degree in business/entrepreneurship and I previously spent the last 6 years working for the Minnesota Twins Baseball team. Although it was a very fun and interesting job, long term career options were limited and I began searching for a different path. I married my wife in September of 2021 and we are expecting our first child on May 24(hopefully not before class ends), these life changes spurred me to move on from my job. The data science centered approach that baseball has taken opened my eyes to the value and utility that coding literacy has in the world today and decided that would be my next move to improve myself and career prospects. I look forward to working with, and learning from all of you.
